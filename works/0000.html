This Website

The Project

I need a website to showcase and link together my internet accounts and personalities. I was also resolved to take something out of quarentine, and wanted to do something more productive in my free time.

The Challenges

The fast-changing opinions and styles required by my client (me) required me to work on this website a lot more then I would have otherwise, which is honestly a big W

I wanted a cheap and effective hosting solution, and settled on Hostinger. After my domain expired, I had a late-night struggle with support to get it renewed for another year, and then eventually transfered it to domain.com (not a promotion) for the cheap-ass price with the exciting TLD .blue

The Work

Over 3 years of spurts of activity, this website has been iterated on and on and on. Last year, with help from .pi, we rewrote the entire thing with a reimagined layout and color scheme. I'm proud of the progress. This is a work of art with a lot more coming to it.
